Why does MyRealMotivation exist
A web application exists to bring together motivated people. The main idea and purpose of the project is the dissemination of information between people, mutual assistance in achieving dreams and motivating people to act.
How to LogIn and to registr
LogIn.
In the upper right corner of the site, click on the login button, it will drop you to the bottom of the first page, where you can go to your profile and enjoy using the web service.
How to registr. For successful registration, you need three steps.
1) In the upper right corner, select the registration button. You have come to a page where you need to enter your name, username, email, which you will need in the future.
2) You will receive a letter with a code on the e-mail, which you will need to enter into the opened form.
3) Create a password. Click Continue. Select the avatar for your profile, set the date of birth, gender and select your totem animal.
